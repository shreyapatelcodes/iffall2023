There It Goes Lyrics

Artist: Maisie Peters

Artist's Note: It’s a song about closure and healing and the power of friendship and flowers and flights and wine fuelled kisses with strangers. it’s a song about coming home to yourself after months of fighting your own shadow, a song about finally being happy exactly where you are, no longer wishing yourself back into a past you couldn’t have even if you wanted it. about realising you don’t really want it anymore anyway. It’s a song about letting go, truly and properly, and the magic that can surround you when you do.
